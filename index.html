Command Centre Revamp

High-fidelity interactive prototype -- April 2026

Design Principles
Information Density
Dense but scannable. Power users handling 20-50+ conversations/day.
AI as Copilot
No separate AI tab. Inline draft replies + embedded copilot in context panel.
Keyboard-First
Cmd+K palette, keyboard shortcuts for all common actions.
Dark-First
Shift workers across Manila/Nairobi/Austin. Toggle available on every screen.
